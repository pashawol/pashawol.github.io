Aktru Mountaineering School

Under the guidance of experienced instructors, and through our expert tour planners, we will help you plan a tour of any difficulty level that you prefer. We have tours ranging from beginner level through advanced, where you will scale the highest mountains of Altai.

You will live in Aktru Mountaineering Camp, which is located in the picturesque valley at the bottom of the glaciers of the North Chuya Range at a height of 2,150 m above sea level. The Aktru area has a lot to offer not just for mountain climbing enthusiasts, but also lovers of unexplored wilderness and beautiful scenery.

All of our instructors-coordinators in mountaineering are qualified by the Mountaineering Federation of Russia to lead sports programs and trainings. You will have a chance not only to train in basics of mountaineering but also to obtain Athletic Category and Official Certificate from Mountaineering Federation of Russia.

Ski-Tours

Aktru Clove is one of the best places in the world for freeride, heliskiing and backcountry. Enjoy downhill skiing and snowboarding on routes of different difficulty levels in the midst of snow-white peaks: Aktru (4 044 m); Karatash (3 534 m); Kzyltash (3 800 m); Snezhnaya (3 800 m); Stazhorov (3 750 m). Experienced instructors team will help you to grasp basic skills. If you are a professional athlete, you will be able to map your own routes in the wild primary mountains of Aktru Clove.

Hunting and Fishing

Additionally, you can go hunting in pristine mountains, and fish clear finny highland lakes. Feel fully connected to nature in the Altai Mountains. You will live in hunting boxes at the height of 2,000m, take trout, peled and grayling, hunt wolves, wild boars, bears and marals, and at the and of an action-packed day, you will rest in a bath on the lake shore admiring the landscape and dining on real fresh food.
